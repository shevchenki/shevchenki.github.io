In Japan, between April and May, We celebrate Independence Day, Green Day, and Children's Day. Along with the weekend, we have five national holidays.

This year, the weather is cooler than other years. I spent time traveling with my wife. We went to Enoshima together. We eat fresh seafood and enjoy life beside the long beach.

I also played soccer with some friends and it was a great time. We were all really into the game, and it was a close match. But in the end, I managed to cross some people and score the winning goal! It was so exciting, and we all celebrated together.

In my 9th year in Japan, my wife and I have a much better life than before. Hope you all had a nice holiday too.
